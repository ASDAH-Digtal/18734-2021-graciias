Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation film studio. The headquarters are located in Koganei, Tokyo. Although producing several short subjects, one television film and television commercials they are mostly popular for their animated feature films. The films have a richly animated image, satisfying color schemes and iconic atmosphere and imagination. The films are based around fantasy and many of the characters are of nothing ever seen before in other films.

List of films produced by Studio Ghibli: Howls Moving Castle, Spirited Away, My Neighbor Totoro, Ponyo, Kiki's Delivery Service, The Wind Rises
